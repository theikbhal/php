# Rejection Muscle — Daily Rejection Therapy Log

A minimal, Twitter-style single-file PHP & SQLite web application built to log daily rejection attempts, desensitize fear responses, and systematically build rejection-handling resilience.

---

## 🎯 Why Rejection Therapy?

> *"If you aren't getting rejected regularly, your goals are too small."*

Rejection Therapy is a habit-building practice designed to overcome social anxiety and fear of failure. By intentionally seeking out rejection, you recondition your nervous system to learn that **"No" is not dangerous**.

1. **Desensitizes Fear:** Reduces emotional spikes when hearing a "No."
2. **Exposes False Expectations:** Reveals that worst-case scenarios rarely happen.
3. **Builds Confidence:** Shifts your focus from outcomes to effort and courage.

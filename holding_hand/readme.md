# 🤝 Holding Hand — Side Idea Park

A minimal, single-file PHP + SQLite web application for capturing sudden thoughts, random ideas, and distraction tasks without breaking deep work flow.

---

## 🎯 Why Holding Hand?

While focusing on your main goal, high-momentum context switches happen when sudden ideas occur.

1. **Zero Context-Switching:** Offload thoughts instantly into a holding buffer.
2. **Delayed Execution:** Keep ideas on "Holding Hand" until your current focus task or 15-minute Pomodoro window opens.
3. **Intentional Processing:** Review parked items during designated break cycles to execute, schedule, or discard.

---
